Things I like in Vermont

If you are my friend, let's consider these things.

Last updated October 2022

  • Flight (with a pilot friend) to Massachusetts Northampton or Turners Falls Airport
  • Flight in a Pipistrel Velis Electro electric airplane
  • Mountain biking around Waterbury VT
  • Prohibition Pig (Waterbury) after a hike/bike
  • Windsurfing out of Burlington Surf Club
  • Swimming in Lake Champlain
  • Pastel sunsets over Lake Champlain
  • Running at Catamount Outdoor Family Center
  • A hike found on the CalTopo App
  • Tennis at Leddy Park
  • Canteen Creemee Company, Waitsfield
  • Day in Montpelier (Bohemian Bakery, Oakes & Evelyn Restaurant)
  • Montreal trip!: Check-out Musée d'art contemporain de Montréal

  • Night ski at Bolton (including Tuesday night "Skimo" skin courses by Catamount Trail Association)
  • Cross-country skiing at Catamount Outdoor Family Center

  • Dinner at Hen of the Wood in Burlington
  • Any night at Wallflower Collective (pool table!)
